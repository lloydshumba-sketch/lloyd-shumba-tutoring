Expert Insights

Why Students Fail AAA

Direct from recent Examiner Reports:

  • Rote Learning: Stating generic risks (e.g., "new client") when the scenario indicates otherwise. Knowledge must be applied to the specific facts.
  • Vague Procedures: Using empty phrases like "check relevant documentation" without specifying *which* document or *why*.
  • Missing the Requirement: Answering "Business Risks" when asked for "Audit Risks".

Current Issue: IFRS 18

Examinable from September 2025:

"Presentation and Disclosure in Financial Statements" replaces IAS 1. The key change is the new structure of the Statement of Profit or Loss, now categorised into:

Operating Investing Financing

It also introduces mandatory disclosure of Management Performance Measures (MPMs) in a single note, ensuring transparency in non-GAAP reporting.
